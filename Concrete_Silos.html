Concrete Silos

Jeddah Additional Silos – JAS Project

Construction of four blocks of concrete silos, each has 20 circular silos & 12 star bins internally connected to the silos, to store 35,000 Ton of wheat, for total storage of 140,000 Tons, in addition to the related Process, and auxiliary facilities.
Project Location: Jeddah – KSA
Year: 2012 – 2014
Client: Grain Silos and Flouring Mills Organization
ASPEN Role: Design, Design Review, Coordination.

Damam Additional Silos – DAS Project

Construction of five blocks of concrete silos, each has 16 circular silos & 9 star bins internally connected to the silos, to store 28,000 Ton of wheat, for total storage of 140,000 Tons, in addition to the related process, and auxiliary Facilities.
Project Location: Damam – KSA
Year: 2012 – 2014
Client: Grain Silos and Flouring Mills Organization.
ASPEN Role: Design, Design Review, Coordination.

AHSA Grain Silos Project

Construction of concrete Grain Storage Silos with capacity 60,000 Ton with all related Process, Auxiliary facilities, and labors colony.
Project Location: AHSA – KSA
Year: 2013 – 2015
Client: Grain Silos and Flouring Mills Organization
ASPEN Role: Design, Design Review, Coordination, and WSD
